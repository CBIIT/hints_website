Help with Graph Image Formats:


Changing the image format changes what functionality is available from a right-click on the image.

JPG: Allows you to obtain a copy of the chart as a JPG. (JPG is a commonly used image format). To save a copy of this chart to your hard drive, right-click on the chart and choose "Save as...".
PNG: Allows you to obtain a copy of the chart as a PNG . (PNG is an image format that typically produces smaller files than GIF or JPG formats). To save a copy of this chart to your hard drive, right-click on the chart and choose "Save as...".
